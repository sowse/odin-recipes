Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • Warm water
  • Active dry yeast
  • Cold water
  • Salt
  • Bread flour
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzarella
  • Oregano
  • Black pepper
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Knead the dough.
  3. Divide in two balls.
  4. Make the pizza skins.
  5. Cover the pizzas with toppings.
  6. Bake in oven.
